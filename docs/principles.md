# Design Axioms

---

## 1. User-Owned Agent

Every person should own their own Agent.
Agents are not platform services.

## 2. Use Over Ownership

Intelligence circulates by use, not by enclosure.
Compute, models, skills, services should not be re-enclosed.

## 3. Public Rules, Private Persons

Rules are public and auditable.
Persons remain private and untraceable.

## 4. Portable Memory

Memory belongs to individuals.
Users can export, migrate, share selectively, or delete.

## 5. Federated Intelligence

Collective intelligence is federated, not centralized.
Individual memory is not forcibly uploaded.

## 6. Compatibility Without Dependence

New protocols interoperate with old systems.
Old systems are resource layers, not sovereignty layers.

## 7. Replacement Over Patching

Do not patch old systems.
Replace their fulcrums with new primitives.

## 8. Anti-Enclosure by Design

Protocol design prevents re-enclosure.
No new platform dependency.
No unnecessary intermediaries.

---

These are constraints, not slogans.
