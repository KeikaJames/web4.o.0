# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Efferva is a protocol project built on principles of openness, sovereignty, and respect.

We are committed to providing a welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

---

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Engage with respect and good faith
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Provide constructive feedback
- Acknowledge different perspectives
- Prioritize protocol clarity over personal preference

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Sharing private information without consent
- Behavior that undermines the project's mission

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Repository interactions (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community spaces (if established)
- Public representation of the project

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to project maintainers.

Reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

Consequences may include:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open-source community standards.
